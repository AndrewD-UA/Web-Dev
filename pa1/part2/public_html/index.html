Warframes: Excalibur

Excalibur Nidus

Description

Excalibur is the cover character for the looter-shooter video game Warframe. Excalibur's primary feature is a widely applicable ability set, average stats across the board without a severe weak spot, and easily recognizable character art. Excalibur excels in close-quarters combat, particularly melee combat. Excalibur is recognizable as the most humanoid of the Warframes, and designed around the original concept of a ninja. Excalibur is one of three Warframes that can be chosen at the beginning of the game in the tutorial, along with Volt and Mag.
