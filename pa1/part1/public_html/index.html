Your knowledgebase on all things Colts

The NFL (National Football League) is a United States-based profesional football organization. Of the 32 teams, objectively the best team is the Indianapolis Colts. The Indianapolis Colts are a team based out of Indianapolis, Indiana at Lucas Oil Stadium. The Colts play in the AFC (American Football Conference) Southern division.

As we approach the beginning of the season, it is important to refresh your memory on who the Colts are. Notable Colts' throughout history include:

  • Peyton Manning (QB)
  • Reggie Wayne (WR)
  • Marvin Harrison (WR)
  • Edgerrin James (RB)
  • Dallas Clark (TE)

These players lead the 2000's era Colts to constant playoff appearances including their 2007 Super Bowl victory.

More recently, the Colts top players include:

  • Quenton Nelson (LG)
  • Shaq Leonard (LB)
  • Deforest Buckner (DT)
  • Jonathan Taylor (RB)

The Colts are also commonly known for their recent draft pick, Anthony Richardson.
